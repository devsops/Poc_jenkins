Bosch respects your privacy

The protection of your privacy in the processing of your personal data is an important concern which we pay special attention to during our business processes. There is no collection & processing of Personal data in this IERO Indoor Positioning Solution – SDK & IERO Admin Application.

 

Collection and processing of data

We process the collected data on confidential basis and strictly in compliance with the Information Technology Act, 2000 and Information Technology (Reasonable security practices and procedures and sensitive personal data or information) Rules, 2011 in India region & the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR") in EU and other regions of the world. In addition, Data protection and information security are an integral part of, and ingrained into, our corporate policy.

 

Analytics Data

The Analytics data shown in this Application is derived from the data that is captured via Indoor Positioning Solution SDK (integrated on a different App operated by the Client’s customers to avail benefits of Indoor Positioning system) – this data is in anonymized form so that it can no longer be traced back to a Client’s customer. In particular, this is done by anonymizing the unique customer identification number used while registering the user requests in Client SDK. This anonymized data is used to derive analytics that can be viewed only in the IERO Admin Application. Only authorized persons (as authorized by the Client) have access to this anonymized data.
